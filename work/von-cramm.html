Cornell Von Cramm Cooperative

Von Cramm Cooperative is a cooperative house at Cornell University with a diverse group of 34 undergraduate, graduate, and international exchange students. Their goal is to be a welcoming and safe community that supports and enriches its members through thoughtful interaction, responsibility, and generosity. The process of design was surprisingly fun due to the variety in layout and my lack of previous experience in this style of website. With almost seven decades of rich history, I wanted to portray a homey, aged, and somewhat rustic feel. I achieved this with the use of an old-style serif font and a musty color palette, which had a combinatory effect that showed age, comfort, and familiarity.
