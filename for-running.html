Running

Pushing the running industry forward with a sustainable experience

Context

Over 23 billion shoes are made worldwide every year. Most are made of plastic and destined for landfills. In 2019, three entrepreneurs from the sporting goods industry wanted to give runners a better kind of running shoe that addressed this issue. In addition to designing a shoe that could meet the needs of the most demanding runners, they wanted to market a sustainable product that would be good for the planet and the generation to come.

Challenge

Launched as a Kickstarter campaign, the product faced a lot of competition in the crowdfunding ecosystem and from big brands already developing new ways to design similar sustainable products. We needed to create a product that was relevant in this era while making it easier for everyday runners to choose great sustainable performance running shoes.
