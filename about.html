Hello! My name is Jacob, and I am a young designer with a passion for creating. Whether it's designing beautiful brand identities or building highly polished user experiences, good design always fills my heart with joy. ♡

About me

I am a senior in high school and a first-generation Korean American.

Since my childhood, I have been intrigued by and exposed to countless subjects and hobbies mostly in the STEM and design fields. For as long as I can remember, I have had a fanatical passion for learning everything that I found to be intriguing, and to satisfy my urges of curiosity, I would spend hours on end probing through the interweb to scavenge every bit of information I could. From graphic design, illustration, game development, animation, and 3D modeling to machining, computer-aided design, woodworking, and architecture, I would always find something new to try to master.

Web design is one of my biggest passions due to the constant loop of problem solving and creativity. Over the past six years, I have developed more than fifteen websites for clients, competitions, and personal activities. Each one has taught me something new, different, and exciting, and it brings me great excitement every time I embark on a new project.
